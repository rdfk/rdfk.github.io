In Loving Memory of Janice M. Schwaiger

February 8, 1933 - August 21, 2016

This page is dedicated to the memory of my grandmother, Janice M. Schwaiger — a woman whose love, strength, and generosity shaped the person I am today.

From a young age, she took me in and raised me as her own, giving me the stability, support, and unconditional love that only a truly selfless heart can offer. She always encouraged my interests, especially when it came to technology. My very first computer — a Packard Bell — was a gift from her, and she made sure I always had access to a computer as I grew. That early support set me on the path to pursuing the passions that still guide me.

This page stands in her honor — a tribute to the love, care, and unwavering dedication she showed me every day of her life. May it serve as a lasting reminder of her kind and nurturing spirit.

Forever loved, forever missed...
