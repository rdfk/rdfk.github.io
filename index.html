About me:

Hey, thanks for stopping by. My name is Rich, and I'm a tech enthusiast. I hope that by visiting this page, you'll get to know me and see some of the projects that I'm working on.

My philosophy for technology is that it should empower the user, and make life simpler by automating monotonous tasks, and increase efficiency. I would like hardware to be upgradable and repairable; to reduce E-Waste and to keep technology affordable for all, especially those who struggle financially.

I'm starting school in January 2026 for Electrical Engineering with a focus on Computer Technology at the ripe age of 44. I hope that I can take the skills that I learn at RCBC and put them to use in creating electronics and writing code that benefits people, and makes their lives easier. I hope to have a large focus on creating hardware and code that helps make life simpler, and safer.

My current areas of focus are web development, Python automation and scripting, and learning the fundamentals of electronics and electronics repair. My hobbies include taking care of my dogs and messing around with SDR to find all different types of signals being broadcast around my area.
